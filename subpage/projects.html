Blender: Furness Railway K2 "Larger Seagull"

During the summer of 2016, I had seen numerous people of the train community create digital working models of locomotives and running them on a simulator called Railworks. Seeing how sucessful they all were, I decided, "how hard could it be?" I downloaded the animating and 3D modeling program known as Blender and made myself a Furness Railway locomotive. However, before I was able to texture it and make the final agreements with the website I was publishing to, the file was lost and all that was left was a screenshot.
